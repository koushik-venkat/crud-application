Post It!

Post It! is a simple webapp where you could post your content through posts.

    User Guide:
  • Click the Add Post button to add the post. Fill the title and content of your post its all set for now to post!!!
  • Click the Get All Posts button to view the posts which were posted by you earlier.
  • Right under the section where you get all the posts you could find buttons for Update Post and Delete Post which enables you to update and delete respectively
